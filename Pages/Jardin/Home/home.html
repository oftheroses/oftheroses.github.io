Gamma Capricorni or γ Capricorni, formally named Nashira,
is a giant star in the constellation of Capricornus.

Based on parallax measurements obtained during the Hipparcos mission,
she is located at a distance of approximately 157 light-years from the Sun.
The star is drifting closer with a radial velocity of −31 km/s. Wikipedia
