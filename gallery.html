Masarap!

‚ô° Ableton
‚ô° Adobe Illustrator + Photoshop

Masarap! is a casual Filipino cooking game that teaches players real recipes & culinary language from all over the Archipelago - think 626 Sandwich Maker, but Filipino!

It is a passion project in which I am the sole artist, musician, and programmer. It's my first real try at game development, and I learned a lot creating it. I also learned digital music productions, as I arranged digital versions of folk songs from all over the countryside. Currently, it teaches Tagalog & Cebuano, though I have plans to add Ilokano and Aklan among other languages.
