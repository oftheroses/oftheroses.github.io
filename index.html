Gamma Capricorni or γ Capricorni, formally named Nashira,
is a giant star in the constellation of Capricornus.

Based on parallax measurements obtained during the Hipparcos mission, she is located at a distance of approximately 157 light-years from the Sun. The star is drifting closer with a radial velocity of −31 km/s. Wikipedia

☀ Hello! I'm Nashira, of Cebu by way of Naarm; currently, a Southerner, for as long as the South exists.

I’m a queer Filipino & Jamaican artist, developer, musician, streamer, and writer, among other things — I’m neurodivergent and following my dopamine wherever it takes me! I'm passionate about experimenting with different mediums to share my experiences.

I started the arts collective Poetry Orchard with my besties where we facilitate creative writing workshops over Zoom. I also stream on Twitch every Saturday & Sunday!

Other topics I'm passionate about include: astrology, disability, history, gender & sexuality, religion & spirituality, as well as motherhood — all intertwined & inseparable from my culture.

I share a new post on Patreon & Substack every week, and I also have a store where I sell prints & stickers.

Irrelevant, but also important: I'm a Capricorn sun, Sagittarius moon, an extrovert, and I'm a contralto!
