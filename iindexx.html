I'm a Jamaican Filipina/Tuvaluan artist who is
passionate about crafting experiences that explore
culture & history, with a focus on Afro-Caribbean,
Southeast Asian & Pacific Islander identity.


My words live in  Marías at Sampaguitas 
and the wonderful  WLWoC Poetry Network
